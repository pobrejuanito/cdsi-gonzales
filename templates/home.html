Powerprox Six Month Braces® Technique

The Powerprox Six Month Braces® Technique is a fast, affordable way to give you the smile you always wanted in just six months – that’s right, six months! The Powerprox Six Month Braces® Technique will move your front teeth to their most beautiful position giving you a great smile. Whether your teeth are crowded together, overlapped, or have spaces between them, the Powerprox Six Month Braces® Technique will give you the smile you always wanted in just six months. This technique was developed by Dr. Richard DePaul, Jr. and we are currently the only one certified around the Monterey Bay area. Want Affordable Dental Benefits?

Did you know that we offer an in-house dental savings plan right here in our practice – for both new and existing patients?
